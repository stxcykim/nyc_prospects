What is our purpose?

Our project, NYC Prospects, is a website built for teenagers like us. The proliferation of alcohol and illicit drugs amongst New York City youth is a seemingly neverending quandary, one which we strive to overcome. Substance abuse typically stems from a desire to conform; teens want a social lubricant, or a chemical refuge from their troubles. However, in indulging in this behavior, teenagers often grow unaware of the endless possibilities that New York City has to offer. We are here to change that! Rather than risking addiction, teens should explore life in other ways. NYC Prospects is a website designed by teens for teens, in hopes that making a networking platform will direct our peers to engaging cultural venues in the city. We hope to lessen the rate of underage substance abuse as well as help to define teenage life differently.
