What is our purpose?

Alcohol and illicit drugs amongst the youth is a seemingly neverending dilemma that we strive to overcome. Substance abuse typically stems from a desire to conform; teens want a social lubricant and want to hide away from their troubles. However, in doing so, teenagers have grown unaware of the endless possibilities that NYC has to offer. We are here to change that! Rather than risking addiction, teens should explore life in other ways. NYC Prospects is a website designed by teens for teens, in hopes that it will direct our fellow peers to the best spots in the city.
